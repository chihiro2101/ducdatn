Ben Johnson will at last get his chance to back up his boasts that he can regain his form of two years ago (1988) when he was stripped of his 100-meter gold medal after testing positive for anabolic steroids.
His two-year suspension ends today and the questions begin -
What can he do when not assisted by drugs?
Promoters of indoor meets this season are anxious to sign him up for high fees in the hope that his promises to redeem himself will fill seats and rejuvenate interest in track.
Johnson has passed at least four drug tests during his suspension.
